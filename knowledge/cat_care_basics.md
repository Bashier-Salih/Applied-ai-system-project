# Cat Care Basics

## Litter box maintenance
Litter boxes should be scooped at least once a day; a full clean (dumping and washing the box) is generally recommended weekly. Multi-cat households typically need one litter box per cat plus one extra to reduce territorial stress.

## Indoor enrichment
Indoor-only cats benefit from daily scheduled play sessions and environmental enrichment (scratching posts, window perches, puzzle feeders) to substitute for the mental stimulation outdoor cats would otherwise get.

## Hairball management
Long-haired cats or heavy shedders may need daily brushing during shedding season to reduce hairball frequency, in addition to the general grooming brushing schedule.

## Hydration
Cats often have a low natural thirst drive, especially on dry food diets. Wet food meals or a water fountain can help maintain hydration, which is relevant when scheduling feeding tasks.
